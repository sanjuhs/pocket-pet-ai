# Pocket Pet AI Brand Mark

The primary symbol combines four ideas in one compact silhouette:

- the rounded enclosure of the pocket compute puck;
- the open loop of the ear-worn interface;
- a subtle lowercase `p` for Pocket Pet;
- a single cobalt pulse representing active local intelligence.

The mark is intentionally quiet and hardware-led. It should feel like a physical product identifier rather than a generic AI logo.

## Assets

- `public/brand/pocket-pet-mark.png` — transparent high-resolution master.
- `public/brand/pocket-pet-mark-256.png` — application and touch icon.
- `public/brand/pocket-pet-mark-64.png` — browser icon.

## Usage

- Use the full near-black and cobalt mark on warm white or pale neutral surfaces.
- Use the monochrome white treatment in the near-black footer.
- Preserve clear space equal to roughly one quarter of the mark’s width.
- Do not rotate, outline, add shadows, place it inside another container, or recolor the cobalt pulse.
- Do not use the mark to imply a finished consumer product; Pocket Pet AI remains open research at concept stage.
